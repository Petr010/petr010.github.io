Denim-Caster Guitar

> This Denim-Caster was made from a reclaimed pallet and a branch found at my local park to form the neck and the body. The side dots were also nails from the pallet. The body of the guitar is dressed with some upcycled jeans and a belt from GoodWill.

> The potentiometer and pickup selector knob were ground down to fit some buttons off of the upcycled jeans.

> Some light soldering was done and some possible filtering may be applied once it is known what LCR circuit best compliments the guitar.

> The original concept was inspired by YouTuber Danny Lewis: https://youtu.be/bXjkmO50csc?si=HkbajuACptsr_Hsl

> The guitar sounds great considering it was the first guitar I've ever made + how the neck was made completely out of pine, a softwood. The frets could definitely use some reworking though.
